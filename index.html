About TREASURE's Hello Tour ───


Find your... TREASURE! ✧

TREASURE is finally coming back to Manila for their 1st Asia Tour, "HELLO," on April 15, 2023 at SM Mall of Asia!


Debuting in 2020, this is their first ever tour, performing outside of South Korea. With their globally viral song, DARARI, TREASURE rose to even more popularity, inviting many international fans to join their fandom. After their Kpop Masterz performance in Manila, this concert is yet another opportunity for Filipino TREASURE MAKERS to meet TREASURE.


With their newest album, The Second Step: Chapter 2, TREASURE MAKERS will get to see them perform their newest songs, like HELLO, HOLD IT IN, THANK YOU, and more!
